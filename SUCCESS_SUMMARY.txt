✨ KEY INNOVATIONS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Gap-Aware MCTS
   └─ UCT formula incorporates gap reduction
   └─ Reward = 0.7 * gap_reduction + 0.3 * quality
   └─ Optimizes for information completeness

2. Multi-Step Reasoning
   └─ Refine Query action
   └─ Expand Entity action
   └─ Verify Answer action
   └─ Explores different paths in search space

3. Knowledge Structuring
   └─ Extracts triples automatically
   └─ Builds graph iteratively
   └─ Quantifies knowledge coverage

4. Explicit Gap Quantification
   └─ Gap metrics show progress
   └─ Demonstrates improvement over baseline
   └─ Enables further optimization
